About The Fashion Pet

The Fashion Pet was created and established in 2020. The owners of the Fashion Pet are animal lovers. They have 3 dogs of their own. Working in the Pet Grooming of a well known Pet Store, they both decided to open thier own Pet Salon. The owners of The Fashion Pet bring years of experience and grooming and taking care of your pet. They also strive in bringing a professioanl personel to the team to go along with their work ehics.

The Fashion Pet is conveniently located in the heart of the city. Living in the same city since a child they have created a very good reputation with pet owners from family, friends, neighbors and new neighbors that just moved in. The Fashion Pet is a pet friendly place where your pet will love to come in and get their grooming services done.
